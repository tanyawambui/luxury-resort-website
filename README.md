# luxury-resort-website

SereneStay is an inland (non-coastal) resort in Kenya that primarily caters to those who have been working too hard for too long and desperately need to relax and unwind. the resort achieves this by creating a tranquil environment for its guests, allowing them to relax and connect with nature. SereneStay does not permit the use of mobile phones, laptops or any other technological gadgets, encouraging guests to disconnect from the outside world.
